Moritz Hartlieb

I am a PhD student at the University of Bonn/MPIM, funded by ERC HyperK. My supervisor is Daniel Huybrechts.

I am interested in algebraic geometry, in particular hyperkähler manifolds, cubic hypersurfaces, moduli spaces and their Brauer groups.
